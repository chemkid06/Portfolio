A lil about me...

Hey there, thanks for checking out my portfolio! For 10 years I’ve worked with and for people in role that makes work life easy and comfortable. Watching the world move to digital this and that made me consider a career change. Now, I’m interested and studying to bring that same ease and comfort to digital interactions. As a Black, lesbian, woman it’s important to me that there be diversity/representation within the design community and a promotion of resources that push equitable design practices. I exist to live in safe and inclusive spaces and given the opportunity to create those spaces for others has brought me into to the UX/UI field.
